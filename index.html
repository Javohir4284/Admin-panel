Yangi Asr Avlodi Publishing and Printing Center was established in 1999. The publishing house operates in two main areas: the publishing plan and the ordered literature. Based on the publishing plan, the publishing house includes a number of areas to meet the needs of today's readers, and these areas have their own goals. More than 500 books published in such sections as "Youth Library", "New Century Literature", "Young Reader", "Pearls of Wisdom", "Key to Success", "Library of World Literature", "Children's Literature" were re-published at the request of readers. is being reprinted.
Yangi Asr Avlodi Publishing House publishes a number of writers and literary scholars, including Hero of Uzbekistan Ibrahim Gafurov, academician Akmal Saidov, Honored Worker of Culture of the Republic of Uzbekistan Khurshid Dostmuhammad, members of the Writers' Union of Uzbekistan Ulugbek Hamdam and Abdukayum Jabbo. has been cooperating closely.
Yangi Asr Avlodi Publishing House regularly publishes 100 books by school authors as part of the Young Reader project for school-age children.
